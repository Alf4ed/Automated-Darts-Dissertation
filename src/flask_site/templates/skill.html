Steps to calculate your skill:

  1. Begin by practicing dart throws, focusing on hitting the inner bullseye.
  2. When you feel ready, click the "Start" button to begin the test.
  3. Enter a name to save your skill profile.
  4. Aim your throws at the center of the dartboard.
  5. Complete 50 throws to assess your skill level.
  6. Once finished, press the "Stop" button to conclude the test.
